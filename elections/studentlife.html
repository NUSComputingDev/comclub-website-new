Events

Computing Freshmen Social Camp (FSC)

FSC marks the start of SOC's very first FOP Event. Freshmen get to immerse themselves in exhilarating outdoor games and sports, while building friendships that lasts beyond the end of the camp. This camp also serves to introduce and expose the freshmen to other FOP events such as Rag & Flag and Bash.

Faculty Orientation Week

A 4-day physical camp where we welcome freshmen into the SoC community and see to the building of new friendships that would follow them through their time at the University and beyond. FOW encourages social interactions between freshmen, as well as with seniors currently in SoC.

BASH

A pageant where 5 pairs of BASHies culminate into one finale night full of fun, laughter, and eyecandy.

E-Facilty Orientation Program (E-FOP)

A 2-day online camp with the main goal of integrating the international freshmen into NUS SoC. Freshies will get the opportunity to make new friends through engaging in exciting activities facilitated online.

Introduction

The Student Relations cell focuses on creating avenues for students to forge new and long lasting connections within SoC. From Computing Day to COMaraderie, we organise a myriad of events to cater to the entire student body. Join the SR cell to look forward to enriching student life here in SoC.

Experience

Working with people from diverse backgrounds has given us new perspectives in the way we problem solve and plan ahead for certain elements in our events! The most fulfilling part would have to be seeing our participants enjoy themselves while engaging in our event activities.

Events

As a cell that serves both supporting and creative roles, we collaborate with all cells in the club closely to help them with their events' publicity materials. Also, we initiate our own events such as social media challenges and video series.

COMaraderie

Pairing participants up, we challenged pairs to embark on a quest to conquer as many challenges as they can, in the hopes of emerging the battle as the top performers to claim attractive prizes.

Meliorium

A one-day cultural event which aims in assimilating international students to the culture here in Singapore. As participants from different nationalities have fun bond together through the activities, we celebrate the cultural diversity here in SoC and promote a vibrant student life here in SoC.

HomeCOMing

Helf in conjunction with the opening of our long awaited COM3, HomeCOMing is back bigger than ever. From carnival games to project showcases by our talented computing students, Homecoming celebrates SoC's presence in the technology sector.
