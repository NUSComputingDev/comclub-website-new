The Events

FSC

A 4-day physical orientation camp organized for the freshmen to form lasting friendships with fellow undergraduates before the official start of university.

eFOP

A 2-day online orientation camp organized for freshmen from the NUS School Of Computing to interact with other students from the faculty. Time will be allotted for information sessions.

RAG

School of Computing's own spin on NUS's flagship charity drive. Owing to COVID-19 concerns, this event only entails the cohesion day without an engagement camp or live performance.

FOW

FOW is a campus wide event where seniors help freshmen adapt to different aspects of university life, through preparatory talks and meeting new friends.

BASH

Bash is a social night event where freshmen and seniors come together to wrap up the entire orientation experience with a night of performances and games.
