FAQ

Q: What are the items I need to prepare?

All those planning will have to submit the nomination form to elections@nuscomputing.com together with their manifesto. If there are no issues with the nomination, candidates can start preparing the publicity materials for their campaign speeches.



Q: What's the commitment?

The amount of commitment varies for every role and also depends on the number of initiative you want to push out. Contact the respective MC members for a clearer understanding of the commitment level. You can reach us on Instagram @nuscomputingclub



Q: What're all the roles in Computing Club?

You can find all the roles and its responsibilities here.



Q: Can I try for multiple roles?

Yes, you can indicate your second choice in your nomination form.



Q: Can i join other ccas with computing club

Yup, you can be part of multiple CCAs in NUS. However, if you are intending to run for President, you cannot be the President of another CCA.
